Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Kirana Bakehouse is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: name, delivery address, email address, phone number (for delivery updates)
  • Payment Information: credit/debit card details (processed securely by our third-party payment processor, Square; we do not store full card numbers)
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on site

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and deliver your subscription orders
  • Send order confirmations, delivery updates, and service communications
  • Improve our website and customer experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Sharing Your Information

We share information only with:

  • Payment processors (as necessary to fulfill orders)
  • Legal authorities when required by law

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Your California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how we use/share it
  • Request deletion of their personal information
  • Opt out of the sale or sharing of their personal information

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last updated" date.

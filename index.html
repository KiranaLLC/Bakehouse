About Us

Kirana Bakehouse is a microbakery based in Menlo Park, specializing in Made-to-Order Artisanal Sourdough.

Each loaf is naturally leavened, slowly fermented, and baked to a deep golden crust in small batches.

No shortcuts. No commercial yeast. Just time, care, and tradition.

Trained at Le Cordon Bleu in Paris, Kirana Bakehouse is rooted in classical technique with a deep love for sourdough, reimagined with inspired, seasonal inclusions and flavors.

At its heart, this is about community. Bread meant to be shared, to bring people together, and to be part of everyday moments.

All of our loaves are Made-to-Order, ~450g each, and require ~3 days of preparation, whether you choose pickup or delivery. We appreciate your patience, it ensures every loaf is baked especially for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does ordering work?

You can order for a PICKUP through Hotplate or DELIVERY through Square.

Do the loaves come pre-sliced?

Yes, we pre-slice them to make it ready to eat, share, or freeze. If you prefer not to have them sliced, please let us know in the order notes.

Do you have a physical location?

No we do not have a physical location currently. We are online only.

Can I make a request for inclusions or submit ideas?

Yes! We'd love to hear your ideas. Please contact us directly through email or Instagram. Although we can't promise to make all of them, we'll definitely consider your suggestions!

What ingredients do you use?

We only use high-quality, organic and, locally sourced ingredients for all our loaves. Our loaves are naturally leavened and DO NOT have preservatives or commercial yeast.

How should I store my bread?

Store your loaf at room temperature in a paper bag or wrapped in a kitchen towel. It is best consumed within 2-3 days. Avoid refrigeration, as it can dry out the bread. If you cannot finish it within a few days, slice your loaf first, then freeze it in an airtight bag, freeze it for up to 1-2 months. When you want to eat it, pop it into a toaster or oven and enjoy!

Do you do catering or bulk orders?

What if I have dietary restrictions or allergies?

All loaves contain gluten (wheat flour). Some include dairy. See full details on the Allergen Info page. Contact us before subscribing if needed.

Is this a licensed bakery?

Yes, we are a licensed California Cottage Food Operation, food safety certified staff, and registered under CFO Permit #26-00019589 in San Mateo County.

Concept

Kirana Bakehouse brings you a monthly bread subscription rooted in slow baking and simple ingredients.

Here’s how it works:

4 weeks. 4 loaves. 1 intentional ritual.

Each week, you’ll receive an artisanal, hand-made, naturally leavened loaf — crafted in small batches, fermented slowly, and baked to a golden crust.

No shortcuts. No commercial yeast. Just time, care, and traditional technique.

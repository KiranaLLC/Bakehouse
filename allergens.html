Allergen & Product Safety Information

Food Allergy Warning: Our breads are handcrafted in a small-batch kitchen and may come into contact with common allergens. We cannot guarantee any product is completely free from allergens or cross-contamination.

Potential Allergens

Our facility/kitchen handles or may have trace amounts of the following:

  • Wheat / Gluten (present in all loaves)
  • Dairy (in some inclusion loaves, if applicable)
  • Nuts (in some inclusion loaves, if applicable)
  • Other allergens depending on seasonal inclusions

Important Safety Notice

Customers with food allergies, intolerances, or medical conditions assume all risk when consuming our products. We strongly advise consulting a physician or allergist before eating if you have known allergies or sensitivities.
